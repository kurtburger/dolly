About Me

Hey y'all! It's me! Dolly! I'm an American singer, songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, record producer, actress, author, businesswoman, and philanthropist, known primarily for my work in country music. After achieving success as a songwriter for others, I made my album debut in 1967, with my album "Hello, I'm Dolly."

With steady success during the remainder of the 1960s (both as a solo artist and with a series of duet albums with Porter Wagoner), my sales and chart peak came during the 1970s and continuing into the 1980s; my subsequent albums in the later part of the 1990s were lower in sales. However, in the new millennium, I achieved commercial success again and has released albums on independent labels since 2000, including albums on my own label, Dolly Records.

Awards

I am the most honored female country performer of all time. Achieving 25 Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) certified Gold, Platinum, and Multi-Platinum awards, I have had 25 songs reach No. 1 on the Billboard country music charts, a record for a female artist (tied with Reba McEntire). I have 41 career top-10 country albums, a record for any artist, and I have 110 career charted singles over the past 40 years. I have garnered nine Grammy Awards, two Academy Award nominations, ten Country Music Association Awards, seven Academy of Country Music Awards, three American Music Awards, and I am one of only seven female artists to win the Country Music Association's Entertainer of the Year Award. I have received 47 Grammy nominations.
